What is Black Friday Battalion?

Black Friday Battalion is a North Hollywood based Black Friday shopping service designed to keep you and other post Thanksgiving shoppers from the nightmare that is all Stores on this particular day. Created by students at the Art Institute Hollywood, Black Friday Battalion covers the entire San Fernando Valley, Hollywood, West Hollywood, and specied areas of Central Los Angeles. The clock is ticking, and Thanksgiving is near, hurry up and book a shopper while they're still available.

How It Works

  1. Go to www.blackfridaybattalion.com and create an acount.
  2. Choose selected stores you wish to have your shopping done at for you as well as items desired.
  3. Select your desired method of delivery of your purchases.
  4. Relax and let your warriors shop!
